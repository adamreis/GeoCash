How does this work?


1. Sign in with Foursquare.

This allows you to find your friends and your favorite locations through GeoCash. We're not going to post anything without your permission, and your information is safe with us!

2. Leave money for a friend.

Using our simple 3-step app, you can leave a gift for a Foursquare friend at a location of your choosing. We use Venmo to help set up a secure transaction, and SendGrid helps us tell your friend about their gift.

3. Your friend checks in on Foursquare.

Your Foursquare friend doesn't have to have a Venmo account - we'll take care of all the details to make sure your gift gets through.

4. Repeat!
